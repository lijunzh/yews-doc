CPIC
----
CNN based models proposed in `Deep learning for seismic phase detection and
picking in the aftershock zone of 2008 Mw7.9 Wenchuan Earthquake <https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0031920118301407>`_ .
Both the models in the original paper (v1) and the simplified model (v2) are
provided.
